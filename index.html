Key Project Findings & Takeaways

Some interesting economic and mobility trends we found in the data

Traffic slows down a lot during rush hour

We found that average taxi speeds drop to around 11 MPH during the morning (8 AM) and evening (6 PM) rush hours because of high traffic. The best time to drive is early morning (5 AM) where speeds go up to 21 MPH.

Card payments are best for tips

People who pay with credit cards tip about 21.8% on average. Cash trips show 0% tips in the data, which is probably because cash tips aren't recorded in the system, or people don't tip with cash as much.

Airport runs are highly profitable

Trips starting or ending at airports (like JFK) have the highest fares and longest distances. These are the most profitable trips for drivers.
